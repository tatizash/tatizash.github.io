Eligibility

But first, let's go over the eligibility requirements. In order to enter the competition, you must:

  • be 24-35 years old;
  • have at least 2 years of professional experience;
  • have obtained a Bachelor's degree or equivalent;
  • be passionate about one or more social issue(s) and demonstrate leadership potential.
